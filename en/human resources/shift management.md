## Shift Management

The Shift Management module in Frappe HR HR helps you efficiently manage shifts for your employees.

Using this module you can:

1.  [Set up a Shift Type](https://docs.erpnext.com/docs/v14/user/manual/en/human-resources/shift_type).
    
2.  [Create Shift Requests](https://docs.erpnext.com/docs/v14/user/manual/en/human-resources/shift_request).
    
3.  [View and Manage Shift Assignments](https://docs.erpnext.com/docs/v14/user/manual/en/human-resources/shift_assignment).
    
4.  [Setup Attendance Device ID field in Employee master](https://docs.erpnext.com/docs/v14/user/manual/en/human-resources/auto-attendance#3-setup-attendance-device-id-field-in-employee).
    
5.  [Create Employee Checkins](https://docs.erpnext.com/docs/v14/user/manual/en/human-resources/employee_checkin).
    
6.  [The system then processes auto attendance if it is enabled](https://docs.erpnext.com/docs/v14/user/manual/en/human-resources/auto-attendance).
