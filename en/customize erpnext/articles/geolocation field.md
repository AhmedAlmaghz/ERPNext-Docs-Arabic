## Geolocation Field

Geolocation is a field type offered by Frappe Framework. This field allows you to insert a field with map, on which you can tag a location or draw as per your need.

This is a field type, majority of the times used in the [Agriculture](https://docs.erpnext.com/docs/v13/user/manual/en/agriculture) module, in the Land and Land Unit master.
